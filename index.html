Computing's effect on social connectivity

Computing has made many contributions to society, but one of the most important is the effect on social connectivity. Thanks to computing (specifically the internet and social media like snapchat and facebook), people are more connected than ever. This increased connectivity has many benefits, a few of these being:
  • Ability to communicate with a wider range of people
  • Decreased cost of long range communication
  • Increase in the strength of social connections
